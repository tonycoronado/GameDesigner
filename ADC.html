A Dragons Call

A Dragon's Call is a fantasy open world prototype game where I worked with two 3D artists and another developer.
Some of the tasks I made:

• Made a playable MVP of 10 min with one main quest, minimal level design and various mechanics.
• Designed and programmed a couple of game systems from scratch.
• Implemented many marketplace assets and solved compatibility issues within them.
• Solved dozens of blueprints errors during development.

Future improvements would be on the quest system, the actual stage is limited and a single NPC cannot assign different missions, or check if a mission is completed after a mission's object is brought to them.
There are some textures that need to be re-done, due to the budget and deadline, they were of low priority/impact.

Tools & resources used:
• Unreal Engine 5
• Autodesk Maya
• Substance 3D Painter

Windows build available on itch.io.
